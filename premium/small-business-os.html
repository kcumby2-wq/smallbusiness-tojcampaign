Small Business OS is high-ticket, high-touch, 12-month minimum. Built for established operators. If any "not for" lines fit you, start Basic and revisit in a year.

◆ Small Business OS is for you if:
  • 5+ years in business
  • 50+ active clients
  • Established personal brand
  • Mid-6-figure annual revenue
  • Trades on relationships (not transactions)
  • Willing to be publicly featured as a case study
  • Ready to commit 12 months for Personal LLM path
◆ Small Business OS is NOT for you if:
  • You're just starting out — go Basic
  • You're a multi-location shop (per-operator only)

  • You want white-label of our tools (not this)
  • You're on a tight budget
  • You want month-to-month with no commitment
  • Your business context is too thin to train on
