The training · Four pillars

Sessions built around the whole body.

Every session integrates all four pillars. Strength drives the outcome. Movement makes the strength real. Conditioning gives you the engine. Nutrition makes it stick.

01

Strength

Barbell, dumbbell, kettlebell. Squat, hinge, press, pull, carry. The five patterns that determine how strong you are at every joint — trained on a progressive plan tuned to your age and current baseline.

02

Movement

Hip mobility, thoracic rotation, shoulder mechanics. FMS-informed corrective work built into every session — so strength lands on a body that can actually use it. Move well before you move a lot.

03

Conditioning

Aerobic base + short-effort intervals. Zone 2 for the base, sled work for the top-end. Built to raise VO2max and recovery capacity, not to smoke you into next Tuesday.

04

Nutrition

Precision Nutrition L1-certified. Protein targets, meal structure, sleep + recovery guidance. Not a diet. A framework for eating like an adult who wants energy tomorrow and progress in six months.
