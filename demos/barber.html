The G.E.M Experience · Plano, TX
"I'm not lucky. I'm prepared."

Fifteen years mastering the craft. Fifteen years watching what one chair, one fade, one honest conversation can do for a man's confidence. The G.E.M Experience isn't a shop — it's a standard. Every seat is set for someone who's ready to become the next version of himself.

Master Barber. Entrepreneur. Mentor. Leader. Builder of People & Legacy. Every one of those titles was earned the same way: by showing up prepared, respecting the craft, and treating every client like the future they're walking into matters.

The G.E.M standard

Work hard. Play smart.

Four values shape every cut, every conversation, every decision made behind Gary's chair — and the men who sit in it.

H
Hard

The kind of hard that shows up before the shop opens and stays after it closes. The kind that keeps sharpening even when the crowd's already convinced. Hard work, no shortcuts.

S
Smart

Every cut is a decision. Every recommendation is calibrated to the man — his face, his job, his stage, his season. Smart work means the work compounds instead of just repeating.

R
Right

Right by the craft. Right by the client. Right by the community. Right by the kid on the sensory spectrum who gets his first haircut without fear. That's the standard.

B
Big

Big heart. Big vision. Big legacy. The G.E.M Experience was never meant to be one chair in one shop — it's meant to be a movement of men returning to themselves, one cut at a time.
