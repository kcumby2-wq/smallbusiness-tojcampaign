# Tier 3 · Small Business OS · The SaaS Program

**The apex tier for operators who want more than a website.**

Every Basic client gets a snapshot. Every Premium client gets a snapshot + system. **Small Business OS clients get an operating system — a private, LLM-powered command center for their entire business, tuned to their voice, that grows into a Personal LLM they control.**

Modeled directly on the Trainer OS pattern for the Personal Business vertical.

---

## What "Small Business OS" is

A private GitHub repo (like [hooks-os](https://github.com/kcumby2-wq/hooks-os) for trainers) built to the six AI Agent Level architecture, adapted for personal-business operators:

| Level | Small Business OS component | What it does |
|-------|----------------------------|--------------|
| **1 · MCP** | `data/` — clients · appointments · products · inventory · leads | Connections to booking, POS, socials, CRM |
| **2 · Single-Agent** | `CLAUDE.md` — full business context | Auto-loads context every session — no re-explanation |
| **3 · Skills** | `skills/` — voice rules · workflows · service frameworks | Reusable capabilities the agent uses on-demand |
| **4 · Multi-Agent** | `workflows/` + service funnels · membership funnels · retail funnels | Specialized agents collaborate on complex ops |
| **5 · Agentic RAG** | `skills/` + `data/` retrieval | Agent decides what to search + how to use it |
| **6 · Memory** | `feedback/` + `improvements.md` | Learns from operator's corrections permanently |

**End state:** a Personal LLM for the operator — trained on their voice, their playbook, their client history — that Kyron controls and maintains.

## What Small Business OS includes

### Everything in Premium Snapshot + System

- Editorial multi-page snapshot website
- Member area / booking system integration
- Client intake system
- Sales flow (tier selector · case studies)
- Monthly content refresh + quarterly strategy review

### Plus · Small Business OS repo (private)

- Custom `{business-slug}-os` GitHub repo built to the Hooks-OS pattern
- Full folder structure: `data/` · `feedback/` · `goals/` · `skills/` · `workflows/`
- Auto-loading `CLAUDE.md` with the operator's business context
- `config.md` end-to-end system configuration
- `improvements.md` audit trail
- Custom platform build plan tuned to the operator's calendar

### Plus · Three included tools (Small Business flavored)

- **Client Insight** — deep client-history summaries + preference tracking + next-appointment recommendations (analogous to Optimum Grading for trainers, adapted for service businesses)
- **Referral Edge** — referral-source tracking + attribution ledger + repeat-customer targeting (analogous to Prospect Edge)
- **Local Directory** — searchable database of local vendors, partner businesses, and complementary services in your area (analogous to College Directory)

### Plus · Personal LLM path

- Progressive tuning of the OS into a Personal LLM
- Feedback loop: operator's corrections → permanent voice rules → the agent gets smarter every month
- 12-month path to a fully trained personal agent that speaks and thinks like the operator

## Who this tier is for

**Ideal Small Business OS customer:**

- **5+ years in business** — enough context for the OS to learn from
- **50+ active clients** — data volume to train on
- **Established personal brand** — voice patterns exist to capture
- **Mid-6-figure annual revenue** — can absorb the price without stress
- **Trades on relationships** — barber · trainer · yoga · realtor · wellness · consultant. Every client is a relationship, not a transaction.
- **Willing to be a case study** — public feature included

**Who this is NOT for:**

- Operators just starting out — go Basic
- Multi-location shops — Small Business OS is per-operator, not per-location
- Anyone who doesn't want an AI system running ops
- Anyone on a tight budget — $8K + $1.2K/mo is real
