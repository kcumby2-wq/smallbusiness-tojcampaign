## Voice signatures — the visual voice

Kyron writes/thinks about design like this:

- **Editorial magazine, not tech-app.** Snapshots feel like features, not landing pages.
- **Anti-flashy.** No gradients-of-doom. No neon. Warm editorial palettes.
- **Type-first.** Anton uppercase carries the display; Fraunces italic accents; Manrope reads clean; JetBrains Mono is the metadata voice.
- **Motion is quiet.** Reveal-on-scroll only. No parallax. No animated backgrounds.
- **Noise is the texture.** Fine turbulence at 0.05-0.08 opacity, `mix-blend: multiply` on warm palettes / `overlay` on dark.
