The System · 6 Levels

Every snapshot is agent-ready.

The intake is structured around the six levels of AI Agents. Every answer you give during intake maps to one of the levels — so the site we build isn't just a brochure, it's the ground floor of an automated business system.

1
◆ Level 01 · MCP
Identity

Who you are, where you're plugged in, what tools you already use. The connection points. Section 1 of your intake covers this level — brand identity, socials, booking system, contact.

2
◆ Level 02 · Single-Agent
Goals

What outcomes should the snapshot autonomously drive? Section 2 defines the single agent goal: bookings, subscriptions, applications, community signups. One primary conversion, focused.

3
◆ Level 03 · Skills
Capabilities

Your actual services, prices, offer ladder. Section 3 is the skill catalog — what you can do, at what price point, in what time. The building blocks for the site's pricing card matrix.

4
◆ Level 04 · Multi-Agent
Team

Who else is in your orbit? Section 4 maps your ecosystem — collaborators, other pros, referral partners, family members in the business. The multi-agent network powering your operation.

5
◆ Level 05 · Agentic RAG
Knowledge

Your body of work — credentials, testimonials, media, content library. Section 5 collects the retrievable knowledge base the snapshot pulls from. Every certificate, every client win, every article.

6
◆ Level 06 · Memory
Continuity

Retention, memberships, ongoing relationships. Section 6 defines how you keep clients over time. The memory layer that turns first cuts into lifetime clients into legacy.
